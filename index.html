Our Services

Comprehensive technical ship management solutions tailored to your fleet's needs

Technical Maintenance & Repairs

Complete technical maintenance programs, planned maintenance systems (PMS), and emergency repair coordination for vessels of all types and sizes. Our experienced technical superintendents ensure optimal performance and regulatory compliance.

Certification & Surveys

Full support for statutory and class surveys, PSC inspections, vetting preparations (SIRE, CDI, RIGHTSHIP), flag and port state compliance. We manage all certification requirements and liaise with classification societies.

Supply Chain Management

Efficient procurement and logistics of spare parts, stores, provisions, and bunkers. Global supplier network, competitive pricing, quality assurance, and timely delivery to any port worldwide.

Drydocking & Major Repairs

Complete drydocking project management from planning to execution. Scope preparation, shipyard selection, specification development, contractor supervision, budget control, and quality assurance throughout the entire drydocking process.

Crew Management Support

Coordination with crew managers, training oversight, seafarer welfare programs, medical management, and ensuring compliance with MLC 2006. Supporting optimal crew performance and retention.

Documentation & Reporting

Comprehensive technical documentation management, regular performance reports, OPEX budgeting and monitoring, condition surveys, and detailed technical specifications. Transparent communication with vessel owners.

Quality & Safety Management

Implementation and maintenance of ISM, ISPS codes, ISO certifications, safety management systems, incident investigation, root cause analysis, and continuous improvement programs to ensure the highest standards.

Performance Monitoring

Fuel efficiency analysis, engine performance monitoring, hull and propeller condition assessment, voyage optimization recommendations, and implementation of energy-saving devices to reduce operational costs.
